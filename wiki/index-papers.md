- [The Faculty of Language: What Is It, Who Has It, and How Did It Evolve? — Hauser, Chomsky & Fitch, Science 2002](wiki/papers/hauser-chomsky-fitch-2002.md) — FLB (broad: sensory-motor + conceptual-intentional + recursion) vs. FLN (narrow: recursion alone, uniquely human); Chomsky hierarchy as a comparative probe (AₙBₙ context-free vs. ABₙ finite-state tamarin result); domain-general-recursion hypothesis (recursion may have evolved for number/navigation/social); discrete infinity ≅ natural numbers; poverty-of-stimulus learnability argument

- [Review of Spelke's What Babies Know — Revencu & Csibra, Mind & Language 2023](wiki/papers/revencu-csibra-what-babies-know-2023.md) — critical commentary: six core domains; modularity + attention-competition as the composition barrier; the natural-language-vs-innate-language-of-thought debate over the composition engine (bidirectional-link failure, combinatorial-explosion double bind, representational-format codec); Stahl & Feigenson non-unitarity of core systems
- [A Natural History of the Human Mind — Sherwood, Subiaul & Zawidzki 2008](wiki/papers/sherwood-natural-history-mind-2008.md) — comparative brain-evolution review; domain-general→domain-specific emergence (reweighted primitives, not new modules); "common code" cross-domain connectivity for generalized systematicity; inhibitory "cognitive self-control" as latent-cause substrate; observable/unobservable-cause decoupling; neoteny numbers (27% brain size at birth); hominid PFC neuromodulatory upgrade
- [Unraveling the evolution of uniquely human cognition — MacLean, PNAS 2016](wiki/papers/maclean-human-cognition-evolution-2016.md) — comparative-cognition framing of "which brain to model": human cognition is a *constellation* of representational + motivational traits (no single system, no single animal has all of it); systemizing-vs-empathizing axis; cumulative culture as the real engine; self-domestication/neoteny as a developmental-timing lever

- [Darwin's Mistake — Penn, Holyoak & Povinelli, BBS 2008](wiki/papers/penn-darwins-mistake-2008.md) — relational reinterpretation (RR) hypothesis: only humans reinterpret first-order perceptual relations as higher-order role-governed structural relations (PSS); conceptual chunking + segmentation (entropy-scalar substitution) as the representational root of shortcut reasoning; functional vs. concatenative compositionality; feature-based vs. role-based rules/categories/systematicity; LISA as the neural "graft" existence proof
- [Transcriptional neoteny in the human brain — Somel et al., PNAS 2009](wiki/papers/somel-transcriptional-neoteny-2009.md) — primary molecular evidence for neoteny: human PFC gene expression is developmentally delayed (adults resemble juvenile chimps); mosaic (~4% of transcriptome), gray-matter/synaptic-specific, peaks at adolescent synaptic pruning → extended plastic window is a selective deferral of the consolidation schedule, not a uniform slowdown
