### 2b. Hierarchical representations missing analogical-reasoning link

`wiki/concepts/hierarchical-representations.md` has no connection to `wiki/concepts/analogical-reasoning.md`. The hierarchical-representations page notes that abstract reasoning requires an additional level beyond perceptual hierarchy; the analogical-reasoning page provides the algorithm that defines what that level must compute. These are direct complements.

**Action:** Add Connection entry in `hierarchical-representations.md` → `analogical-reasoning.md` (and reciprocally): "hierarchical-representations describes the perceptual substrate (feedforward hierarchy) whose output is the input for analogical relational structure-building; the CLSU-level identity factorization is the prerequisite for the role-filler binding that analogy requires."

### 2c. Successor representation page missing connections

Given that SR/grid-cell equivalence is central to TEM and CSCG, these are obvious missing links.

**Action:** Update `successor-representation.md` related list and Connections section to include at minimum: `tem-model.md`, `cscg-model.md`, `whittington-cognitive-map-2022.md`, `tem-whittington-2020.md`.

### 2d. convergent-allocentric-coding.md not linked from recent concept pages

`wiki/concepts/convergent-allocentric-coding.md` is the master comparative hub for convergent evolution evidence, but it is not referenced by several key pages added in the 2026-06-20 batch:
