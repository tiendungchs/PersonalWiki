# The Prefrontal Cortex: Categories, Concepts and Cognition

**Miller, E. K., Freedman, D. J. & Wallis, J. D. (2002). *Phil. Trans. R. Soc. Lond. B* 357, 1123–1136.** The empirical companion to Miller & Cohen (2001): single-neuron evidence that PFC represents the abstract "rules of the game."

## Key computational insights

- **Category tuning carved into single neurons (Freedman et al. 2001, cat/dog DMC).** A 3-D morph continuum was split into two categories with a sharp boundary. After training, lateral PFC neurons treated dog-like cats (60/40) like prototype cats and vice versa — *categorical* (sharp-boundary), not gradual similarity tuning. Population tuning shifted toward category over individual stimulus. Retraining on three *orthogonal* category boundaries shifted neural activity to the new — not the old — scheme: category membership is learned into tuning, not innate.
- **Abstract rules generalize to novel stimuli (Wallis et al. 2001, match/non-match).** Single PFC neurons encoded the currently-active rule independent of cue modality, remembered picture, forthcoming response, and reward expectation. Monkeys performed above chance on *first-ever-seen* stimuli — the minimal definition of an abstract rule. Framed explicitly as **compression / learning shortcut**: 2 rules replace 16 cue-specific paired associates; a rule abstracted over modalities avoids a new cell population per modality.
- **Nonlinear conjunctive tuning = origin of mixed selectivity (Asaad et al. 1998).** ~44–50% of lateral PFC neurons show conjunctive object×saccade tuning not predictable from single-factor responses. Rule-specific baseline shifts flag which task is active.
- **Rapid plasticity.** Conditional associations learned in 5–15 trials; response-related delay activity migrates *earlier in time* across trials (toward cue onset) as the association is learned. FEF neurons acquire target-feature selectivity within minutes.
- **Sustained activity as a distinct representational format.** Miller & Cohen model: information held in sustained activity (vs. synaptic weights) is globally broadcastable and instantly re-configurable — the format cognitive control needs to orchestrate many brain systems. Capacity limit of controlled processing follows from population-code interference. Task model = a "map of the neural pathways" needed to solve the task; VTA dopamine bootstraps it from reward.

## Limitations

- Correlational single-unit recording in 2 monkeys; category-vs-ITC contribution unresolved (ITC may store, PFC retrieve).
- Rules tested are shallow (match/non-match); no multi-level rule nesting or compositional chains.
- Pre-dates the CCGP/SD geometric framework — "conjunctive tuning" is described qualitatively, not as population geometry.
